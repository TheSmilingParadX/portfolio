Profile

In my work, I find myself obsessing over masks and how we use them to either access another personality that we rarely use or become a concept foreign to others. In my art, these “masks” take the form of fantastical characters I have created recently as my pieces tell their own stories through the use of 3D modeling software, such as Blender, and 2D digital art. Although, I do plan to experiment with other mediums if it means to see my favored ideas come to life.

I was born and raised in San Jose, California. I am currently studying at San Jose State University as an undergraduate BFA Digital Media Art major with a minor in Architectural Studies.
